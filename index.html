Assessment Framework
What We Measure

Scientific temper goes far beyond memorising facts. Our framework maps six core dimensions of scientific thinking, from basic literacy to the disposition for evidence-based reasoning.

🔬
Conceptual Literacy
Understanding of core scientific concepts across physics, biology, chemistry, and earth sciences.
🧠
Critical Thinking
Ability to evaluate arguments, identify logical fallacies, and assess the quality of evidence.
📊
Statistical Reasoning
Interpretation of data, understanding of probability, risk, and correlation vs causation.
⚗️
Scientific Method
Knowledge of hypothesis testing, experimental design, peer review, and replication.
🌐
Science-Society Interface
Awareness of how science informs policy, technology, medicine, and environmental decisions.
🛡️
Misinformation Resistance
Ability to identify pseudoscience, conspiracy thinking, and misinformation patterns.
